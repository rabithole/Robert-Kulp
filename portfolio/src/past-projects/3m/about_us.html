Triple M Alpacas is located in Washougal, Washington in the foothills of the Cascades, near the mouth of the Columbia River Gorge. Triple M started back in the 1940's, as a dairy, owned by Buds grandfather, JC, we wanted to continue with the family name. We started this industry with 3 Alpaca females, we succeeded in growing our herd, and continue to specialize in the darker colored animals, along with improving the density and fineness of the fleece. Our goal is to help others get started in this business, and help them succeed.
